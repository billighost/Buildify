The Journey

Our Story

From a simple idea to revolutionizing construction across Nigeria

2020

The Beginning

Founded by Adebayo Hassan after witnessing the challenges faced by his family during their home construction project. The idea for Buildify was born from the need for accurate block calculations and cost estimates.

2021

First Prototype

Developed the initial algorithm for block calculations and launched the first version serving 50 early users in Lagos. Received positive feedback and began refining the technology.

2022

Expansion & Growth

Expanded to 5 major Nigerian cities. Integrated real-time price data from construction markets. Reached 1,000 active users and helped save over â‚¦50 million in construction costs.

2023

Platform Launch

Launched the full Buildify platform with advanced features including AI-powered calculations, professional reporting, and mobile optimization. Grew to 10,000+ users across 15 states.

2024

Building the Future

Continuing to innovate with new features, expanding our team, and working towards making construction planning accessible to every Nigerian. Our goal is to become the go-to platform for construction planning in Africa.
