Introduction

At Buildify, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our construction planning platform.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Use our construction planning tools
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact our support team
  • Participate in surveys or feedback

This may include:

  • Name and contact information
  • Professional details (for contractors)
  • Project specifications and construction data
  • Payment information (for premium plans)
Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our platform:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Error logs and performance data

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

To provide and maintain our construction planning services, including block calculations and cost estimates.

Service Improvement

To enhance and optimize our algorithms and user experience based on usage patterns.

Security & Protection

To protect against fraudulent activities and ensure platform security.

Communication

To send important updates, security alerts, and support messages.

Analytics

To analyze usage trends and improve our service offerings.

Marketing

To send promotional communications (with your consent).

Data Protection & Security

Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
Access Controls: Strict role-based access to sensitive data
Secure Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade cloud security
Regular Audits: Continuous security monitoring and testing
Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training

While we implement appropriate security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request copies of your personal data

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate data

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data

Right to Restrict

Request restriction of data processing

Right to Portability

Request transfer of your data to another service

Right to Object

Object to our processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@buildify.ng. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our platform and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

Types of Cookies We Use
Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic platform functionality Session
Performance Cookies Help us understand how users interact with our platform 1 Year
Functionality Cookies Remember your preferences and settings 1 Year
Advertising Cookies Deliver relevant advertisements (opt-in) 90 Days

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.
